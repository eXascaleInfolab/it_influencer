Overview

We are researchers from the University of Fribourg. The goal of our research is to predict fashion trends. To achieve this goal, we need to find fashion influencers on Twitter as they have a strong impact on the fashion industry. In this task, you will help us find these fashion influencers on Twitter. We will ask you to name as many fashion influencer as you know.

Steps

This task consists of two main sections:

  1. The first section is designed to get to know you a little which will help us to analyse the results we get. We will ask you general questions about your age, gender and location. Your answers will be kept anonymous
  2. The second section is related to your knowledge about fashion influencers and fashion in general.

Rules and Tips

  • By participating in this task you will help our academic research. If you continue you accept our Terms and Conditions.
  • A fashion infuencer is someone who has an impact on his/her followers if he/she promotes new products or a new fashion style.

IMPORTANT:

  • You need to validate both Section 1 and Section 2 to be able to submit the survey.

  • When you finish all sections of this survey, click "Submit" to submit yours answers.

  • You need to be signed in to your twitter account to work on the task

    • The fashion influencer and your follower's account need both to be valid twitter accounts. To ensure this, you might want to log in to your account so you get the correct user names otherwise you would likely get an error message.
