Overview

We are researchers from the University of Fribourg. The goal of our research is to predict Information technologie (IT) new trends. To achieve this goal, we need to find IT influencers on Twitter. In this task, you will help us to find these IT influencers on Twitter. We will ask you to name as many IT influencers as you know.

Steps

This task consists of two main sections:

  1. The first section is designed to get to know you a little which will help us to analyse the results we get. We will ask you general questions about your age, gender and location. Your answers will be kept anonymous
  2. The second section is related to your knowledge about IT influencers and IT in general. We will ask you to name a few IT influencers

Rules and Tips

  • By participating in this task you will help our academic research. If you continue you accept our Terms and Conditions.
  • An IT infuencer is someone who has an impact on his/her followers if he/she promotes new products or a new technology.

IMPORTANT:

  • You need to validate both Section 1 and Section 2 to be able to submit the survey.

  • When you finish all sections of this survey, click "Submit" to submit yours answers.

  • You need to be signed in to your twitter account to work on the task

    • The IT influencer and your account need both to be valid twitter accounts. To ensure this, you might want to log in to your account so you get the correct user names otherwise you would likely get an error message.
